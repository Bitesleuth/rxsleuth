RxSleuth

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026  ·  Effective date: July 2026

🔒 The short version: RxSleuth does not sell your health data, does not show you ads, and only collects what is necessary to make the app work. Your medication and health data is yours alone.

1. Who We Are

RxSleuth is operated by Vanessa Nix, Trading as BiteSleuth, based in North Carolina, United States. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at privacy@rxsleuth.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the RxSleuth service:

  • Account information: Your email address when you create an account
  • Medication data: Medication names, doses, frequencies, and prescriber information you enter
  • Health logs: Symptoms, severity ratings, and notes you log in the app
  • Vitals data: Blood pressure, heart rate, blood glucose, and weight readings you enter
  • Appointment information: Doctor names, appointment dates, and locations you add
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe — we never see or store your full card details

We do not collect your location, contacts, device identifiers, or any data unrelated to the app's core health tracking function.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the RxSleuth service:

  • To log and display your medications, symptoms, and vitals
  • To generate AI-powered doctor visit summary reports (premium feature)
  • To sync your data securely across your devices
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To send medication reminders and appointment notifications
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not use your health data for advertising, marketing profiling, research, or any purpose beyond making RxSleuth work for you.

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your data to anyone, ever. We share limited data only with the following trusted service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Supabase — secure cloud database and authentication provider
  • Stripe — payment processing (they handle your card details, we never see them)
  • Anthropic — powers the AI Doctor Report feature; your health summary data is sent to generate your doctor visit report
  • Netlify — hosts the app and its backend functions

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy and security practices. We do not share your health data with advertisers, pharmaceutical companies, data brokers, insurers, or any third parties for commercial purposes.

5. AI Doctor Report Feature

When you generate an AI Doctor Report (premium feature), a summary of your health data including medications, symptoms, and vitals is sent to Anthropic's API to generate your personalized report. This data is processed according to Anthropic's privacy policy. We do not store the content of your AI report requests beyond what is necessary to display results in the app. Your health data is never used to train AI models.

6. Important Health Data Disclaimer

RxSleuth is a personal health tracking tool and is not a medical device. The information in the app, including AI-generated reports, is for personal reference only and does not constitute medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions. Do not rely solely on RxSleuth for medical diagnosis or treatment.

7. Data Security

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS. Your account data is stored securely in Supabase with industry-standard encryption. We implement Row Level Security on our database, meaning your health data can only be accessed by you when you are signed in to your account. No one else — including our team — can access your personal health records.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all of your personal data including medication logs, health records, vitals, and account information will be permanently deleted from our systems. You can delete your account at any time from the Account section of the app.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the health data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (delete your account in the app)
  • Export your data (CSV export feature in the app)
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@rxsleuth.com.

10. Children's Privacy

RxSleuth is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at privacy@rxsleuth.com.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@rxsleuth.com.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of RxSleuth after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
